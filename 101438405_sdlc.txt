Learning DevOps is indispensable for software engineers due to its pivotal role in streamlining the software development lifecycle. DevOps practices integrate development and operations, fostering collaboration and efficiency.
DevOps speeds feedback and development iterations. This lets developers deliver features and updates faster and more often. 
Using testing, validation, verification, security, performance, availability, scalability, and resilience throughout the software development process, DevOps improves product quality and reliability.
