George Brown College opened its doors in 1968, welcoming just over 2,000 students.
In 2002 George Brown partners with Ryerson University to offer collaborative programs in areas such as Nursing and Early Childhood Education.
George brown was named after a politician who played a crucial part in the Confederation. He was a reformer who brought responsible governance to Upper Canada and was the mastermind behind the remarkable coalition of 1864 that drove British North America in the direction of Confederation.
